The Company's Articles of Association specify the number of members of the Board of Directors as being nine. This is in line with the Corporate Governance Regulations issued by the Capital Market Authority (CMA). A new Board of Directors was elected on 28/4/2018 for the seventh session for a period of three calendar years. The following tables list the names of the members of the Board and their memberships in boards of directors in joint-stock companies listed or not in the financial market, in the Kingdom or abroad, along with a description of their portfolios.
